Classic Pancakes

A simple, fluffy pancake recipe tested and rewritten for perfect weekend brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently; lumps are okay.
  4. Cook pancakes on a hot pan until golden on both sides.
